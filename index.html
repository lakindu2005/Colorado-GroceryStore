Colorado Grocery Store is a grocery delivery and pickup service that offers products such as vegetables, dairy, and cosmetics. Customers can have their orders delivered to their doorstep or pick them up at a local Colorado Grocery Store.


Colorado Grocery Store's history began in 2012 when Lakindu Kaveesha launched the first store in Denver, Colorado. Later in 2018, the store has expanded to Glenwood Springs, Colorado.
